About

Our journey so far.

  • December 2016

    CULTS is founded at Cambridge

    Conceived by an ambitious team of students, CULTS is created at the university with the goal to promote the integration of law and technology and to empower students to leverage technology to their advantage.

  • January 2017

    CULTS launch

    After managing the organizational and bureaucratic hurdles, we hope that CULTS will be ready to launch by the end of January. Stay tuned for more updates on our social media!

  • March 2017

    First Legal Logic Session with Reed Smith

    We are currently coordinating our first event with Reed Smith in March. The event will consist of a weekend-long legal logic session where students will be confronted with a legal problem and given a specific amount of time and technologically valuable tools to solve it.

  • April 2017

    Second Legal Technology Session

    Before the end of the academic year, we aim to host a second legal technology session, most likely in the form of a hackathon. This event will introduce students to the potential for web-apps and software to automatize and facilitate access to justice.

CULTS x Reed Smith

The Logical Structure of the Law

This event will take place over an entire weekend in Cambridge and aims to empower students to understand the logical structure behind the law. Reed Smith lawyers will provide firsthand insights and access to highly sophisticated AI and LegalTech tools such as ROSS Intelligence or Neota Logic. The goal of this session is to provide students with the skills they will need as aspiring commercial lawyers in the 21st century.
